Built to know what your plants need

Different plants have different needs. Some need watering weekly. Some need it monthly. Then in the winter, who even knows! Water My Plants 2 knows - and we help you figure it out. Create a list of the plants you own, update when you water them, and we'll send you reminders when they need it next.

Plants also require differing amounts of sunlight. Have a great southern exposure that you want to throw a couple of Pothos in? Maybe a bookshelf in your living room that doesn't get direct sunlight? With Water My Plants 2's roomscanner technology, we'll build a virutal floor plan of your home, orient it properly with the sun, and suggest placement for all of your greren buddies.

And connect securely to your smart home

A modern home is a connected home. Water My Plants 2 integrates seamlessly with most smart lighting systems, allowing you to tweak your grow lamps from afar. Or, ask Alexa to check on the lighting in your dining room from the comfort of your sofa, and adjust as Water My Plants 2 suggests.

All communications between Water My Plants 2 and the IOT are cryptographically secured, ensuring that the details of your home's interior, and your plant inventory, remain a secret. Biometric security precautions and local storage mean that that info doesn't leave your phone unless you want it to.
